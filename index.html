01 — Welcome

For the
first-timer.
For the lifer.

Everyone who walks in has a different story. Some are just beginning, some are coming back after years away, and some have been on the mats since before they can remember. We built Lost Park for all of them.

For new students, that first year is everything — the one that decides whether jiu jitsu becomes part of your life or just a thing you tried once. We pour extra energy into beginners so the answer is the first one. Classes are structured for fundamentals, intermediate, and advanced students, so wherever you are, you're in the right place.

For longtime grapplers, this is a place to loosen up, ask questions, get beat up, solve problems on the mat and in life. To get lost. To find something again.

First class is free. Come see what we're building.

Who we are.
